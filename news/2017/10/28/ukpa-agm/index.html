UK Python Association AGM

28th October 2017

The first AGM of the UK Python Association was held during PyCon UK on Saturday 28th October.

The following were elected by acclamation to serve as trustees of the board:

  • Chloe Parkes
  • Kirk Northrup
  • Kristian Glass
  • Nicholas Tollervey
  • Owen Campbell
  • Peter Inglesby
