John Pinner Award recipients

16th September 2018

The John Pinner Award celebrates voluntary service to the Python community in the UK.

During PyCon UK, the following members of our community were presented with a medal by Sheila Pinner:

  • Alan O'Donohoe
  • Ben Nuttall
  • Claire Wicher
  • Dave Jones
  • Kirk Northrop
  • Les Pounder
  • Mario Corchero
  • Tania Allard
  • The London Python Dojo team
  • Tim Williams

Congratulations to the recipients!
